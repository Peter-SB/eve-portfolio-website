### Overall vibe:

* Playful
* Friendly

* Slightly “handcrafted” / indie brand feel

### Design traits:

* Rounded everything (text, shapes, icons)
* Low contrast backgrounds (easy on the eyes)
* Flat design (no heavy shadows)
* Pastel color blocking

👉 This is very “modern cute SaaS / lifestyle brand”

This style works best if you:
